Poppy Paulus-Nicolas

Poppy Paulus-Nicolas is an artist and curator. In her art practice she takes on the role of reporter or observer, exploring how text and image can be repurposed to create new narratives, and weaving multi layered simulacrums. She has worked stints at Josilda da Conceição, Torch and Corridor, as well as co-founding the temporary project space Dapper. She is a recent graduate of the Gerrit Rietveld Academie, and is based between NL/UK.
